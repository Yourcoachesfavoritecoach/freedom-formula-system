Freedom Formula - Client Onboarding

Add a new client to the system after setting up their GHL sub-account.

Before You Start

  1. Create the client's GHL sub-account under the Fit Biz Solutions agency.
  2. Generate a Private Integration Token (PIT) in the sub-account with all scopes enabled.
  3. Create a contact in the client's sub-account for the business owner.
  4. Fill in the form below with those details.
  5. Click "Onboard Client" - the system handles the rest (creates mirror contact, syncs fields, etc.).
