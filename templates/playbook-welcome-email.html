What is the Playbook?

The Playbook is your personal command center for the program. Your weekly health score, coaching action items, business metrics, milestones, reading list, and schedule all live in one place.

No more guessing where you stand. No more digging through texts or emails. Every number, every assignment, every win is tracked here automatically so you can focus on executing.

■ Weekly Health Score
One number. Know exactly where you stand every week.
■ Coaching Action Plan
Every assignment from your coaching calls, tracked with deadlines.
■ Business Metrics & Milestones
Revenue, leads, members, conversions, and your 30/60/90 day wins.
■ Book Library & Resources
Your curated reading path plus recordings, templates, and SOPs.
